McKinley Family Meals: Week 3

Blackstone Caprese Sliders Blackstone & Basil

Morning Prep Checklist

  • Thaw: Ensure 1 lb chicken breast is ready.
  • Chop: Slice chicken into slider-sized squares. Slice mozzarella and halve grape tomatoes.
  • Harvest: Pluck 12 basil leaves.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat Blackstone to medium-high. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Grill chicken 4-5 mins per side. Add mozzarella during the last 2 mins to melt.
  3. Toast slider buns.
  4. Assembly: Bun bottom > Chicken with cheese > Basil leaf > Tomato > Balsamic glaze > Top bun.
For Mason: Serve deconstructed (chicken bites, cheese cubes, tomato slices).

One-Pan Lemon Butter Chicken & Orzo Indoor Only

Morning Prep Checklist

  • Thaw: Ensure 1 lb chicken breast is thawed.
  • Chop: Dice chicken into bite-sized pieces. Mince 3 cloves of garlic.
  • Zest: Zest and juice 1 lemon.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, sauté diced chicken until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add a bit of butter and sauté garlic for 30 seconds. Add 1 cup dry orzo and toast for 1 minute.
  3. Add 2 cups chicken broth and lemon juice. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for 8-10 mins (until orzo is tender).
  4. Stir in chicken, 1/4 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup parmesan, and lemon zest. Stir until creamy.
For Mason: This is basically a "creamy rice" texture. Ensure it's not too hot before serving.

Sheet Pan Honey-Mustard Salmon Oven Only

Morning Prep Checklist

  • Thaw: Ensure salmon fillets are thawed.
  • Chop: Halve baby potatoes and trim green beans.
  • Mix: Whisk 2 tbsp honey and 1 tbsp Dijon mustard.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper on a sheet pan. Roast for 15 mins.
  3. Add salmon and green beans to the pan. Brush salmon with honey-mustard.
  4. Roast for 10-12 mins until salmon flakes with a fork.
For Mason: Flake the salmon into bites. Serve green beans as "fries."
